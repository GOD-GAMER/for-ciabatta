Create Twitch Application

  1. Go to the Twitch Developer Console
  2. Click "Register Your Application"
  3. Fill out the form:
    • Name: Your Bot Name (e.g., "MyBakeBot")
    • OAuth Redirect URLs: https://localhost
    • Category: Chat Bot
  4. Click "Create" to create your application

Get Authorization

  1. Click "Generate URL" below to create the OAuth URL
  2. Click "Open in Browser" to go to the authorization page
  3. Sign in with your BOT account (not your main account)
  4. Click "Authorize" to grant permissions
  5. Copy the access_token from the URL (after #access_token=)
